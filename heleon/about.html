Big Island Buses

Go Green. Go Hele-On. Go Online.

Stop fumbling with paper schedules and never miss the Hawaii bus again! Use Big Island Buses, a brand-new app created in partnership with Hele-On. Find bus stops near you, see live GPS locations of buses, and look for the schedules from the comfort of your phone.

Coming out this month.

Discover stops with ease

Wondering where the bus runs? Stop rummaging through the numerous paper schedules. See every bus stop on an interactive map, automatically find the bus stop nearest you, and find stops near attractions or places. You can even see which bus routes run through each stop.

Avaliable everywhere

Access the app anywhere, by visiting goride.io. Use it on any device with an internet browser, including your laptop, smartphone, tablet, and even your TV.

Hele-On and Google Maps. Together at last.

Public transit directions are now avaliable for the Big Island on Google Maps. Find directions via Hele-On bus with your favorite map tool.

Never miss a bus off-schedule

Big Island Buses will be the first application to provide live bus locations for the Hele-On bus. Don't let buses arriving off-schedule deter you; use live updates to keep track of altered arrival times.

Live data may not be avaliable at launch, but will come soon.
