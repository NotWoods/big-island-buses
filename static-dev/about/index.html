Meet the new app for the Hele-On bus schedules

Stop fumbling with paper schedules and never miss the Hawaii bus again! Use Big Island Buses, a brand-new app created in partnership with Hele-On. Try it now >

Discover stops with ease

Wondering where the bus runs? See every bus stop on an interactive map, automatically find the bus stop nearest you, and find stops near attractions or places. You can even see which bus routes run through each stop.

Available everywhere

Access the app anywhere by visiting goride.io. Use it on any device with an internet browser, including your laptop, tablet, and smartphone. Even when you’re offline, you can access the bus schedules.

More to come

We’re working to bring live bus locations to the app. Live updates will let you keep track of altered arrival times; never let off-schedules buses deter you again.
Plus, transit direction for the Big Island will soon be available in Google Maps.
